How to choose the right template

Start with relevance and readability. Recruiters skim in seconds, so favor clear headings, sensible spacing, and a hierarchy that highlights impact. All ResumaticAI templates are designed to parse reliably in most Applicant Tracking Systems, and export cleanly to PDF and DOCX. Use color accents sparingly unless you are targeting creative roles. When in doubt, Classic styles are a safe bet; Modern adds polish; Creative works well when design is part of the job.
