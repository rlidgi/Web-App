1. Chronological Format (Most Common)

  • ✅ Best for: steady career paths, traditional industries
  • 🗂️ Structure: Work experience in reverse chronological order
  • 📈 Strength: Shows career progression clearly

2. Functional Format

  • ✅ Best for: career changers, gaps in work history
  • 🧰 Structure: Skills and accomplishments first, experience last
  • 🎯 Strength: Highlights transferable skills, downplays gaps

3. Hybrid Format

  • ✅ Best for: most modern professionals
  • 📎 Structure: Combines summary, skills, and experience
  • ⚖️ Strength: Balances flexibility with depth

What to Avoid

❌ Creative resumes with heavy visuals, columns, or embedded images

❌ Unconventional headings or file types (e.g., .pages, .txt)

Tips for Formatting Your Resume

  • 🗓️ Use consistent formatting for dates and locations
  • 📌 Use bullet points — not paragraphs
  • 🔤 Stick to professional fonts like Arial, Calibri, or Times New Roman
